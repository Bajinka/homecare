About Born to Excel PLLC

Our Facility

Born to Excel PLLC is a licensed behavioral health residential facility located in Arizona, USA, dedicated to providing safe, structured, and compassionate care for youth ages 14 and under with behavioral health needs. Our facility operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring continuous supervision and support for all residents.

24/7 Supervised Care

Our professional staff provides round-the-clock supervision in a therapeutic environment designed to promote healing, growth, and development. We maintain a low staff-to-resident ratio to ensure personalized attention and immediate response to individual needs.

Licensing and Compliance

We are fully licensed and compliant with Arizona Department of Health Services (ADHS) and Arizona Health Care Cost Containment System (AHCCCS) standards. Our facility undergoes regular inspections and maintains the highest standards of care and safety.

Our Mission

To provide safe, structured, and compassionate behavioral health residential care for youth ages 14 and under, fostering healing, growth, and successful community reintegration in compliance with Arizona's ADHS and AHCCCS standards.

Our Vision

To be Arizona's leading behavioral health residential facility, recognized for excellence in child-centered care, therapeutic interventions, and measurable outcomes that transform lives.
