Safe, Structured, and Compassionate Behavioral Health Care for Youth

Born to Excel PLLC provides 24-hour supervised residential care for youth ages 14 and under with behavioral health needs. Our Arizona-based facility follows ADHS and AHCCCS standards to ensure the highest quality of care.

Contact Us Learn More

Our Services

24/7 Supervision & Safety

Round-the-clock professional supervision ensuring the safety and well-being of all residents.

Behavioral Health Support

Comprehensive behavioral interventions, counseling, and crisis support tailored to each youth's needs.

Medication Management

Professional medication monitoring and coordination with healthcare providers.

Daily Living Skills

Structured programs to develop essential life skills and promote independence.

Emotional Development

Social and emotional learning programs to build resilience and healthy relationships.

Community Integration

Safe community outings and activities to promote successful reintegration.

Our Mission

To provide safe, structured, and compassionate behavioral health residential care for youth ages 14 and under, fostering healing, growth, and successful community reintegration in compliance with Arizona's ADHS and AHCCCS standards.

Our Vision

To be Arizona's leading behavioral health residential facility, recognized for excellence in child-centered care, therapeutic interventions, and measurable outcomes that transform lives.
